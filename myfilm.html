My Kind of Films

NOW SHOWING: TV SHOWS

Gossip Girl

Drama, Teen

Set in New York’s Upper East Side, Gossip Girl follows the lives of wealthy high school students attending elite private schools. Their glamorous yet scandalous lives are exposed by an anonymous blogger known as "Gossip Girl", narrated by Kristen Bell. The series mainly focuses on Serena van der Woodsen (Blake Lively) and Blair Waldorf (Leighton Meester), along with their romantic entanglements, friendships, and betrayals.

Grey's Anatomy

Medical, Drama

"Grey’s Anatomy" follows the lives of surgical interns, residents, and attending doctors at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital as they navigate their medical careers and personal relationships. The series centers around Meredith Grey, played by Ellen Pompeo, and showcases the emotional, ethical, and professional challenges faced in a high-stakes hospital environment.

NOW SHOWING: MOVIES

10 Things I Hate About You

Romance, Comedy

A modern retelling of Shakespeare’s "The Taming of the Shrew," this film follows Kat Stratford, a smart and opinionated high school student who refuses to date. However, her younger sister, Bianca, can’t date until Kat does. Enter Patrick Verona (Heath Ledger), a rebellious yet charming outsider, who is bribed to woo Kat. What starts as a setup turns into a genuine love story, complete with one of the most romantic movie scenes ever—Patrick singing "Can’t Take My Eyes Off You."

Legally Blonde

Comedy, Drama

Legally Blonde follows Elle Woods, a fashionable and bubbly sorority girl who gets dumped by her boyfriend, Warner Huntington III, for not being "serious enough." Determined to win him back, Elle applies to Harvard Law School—and gets in! But instead of chasing Warner, Elle discovers her own intelligence, strength, and passion for law, proving that you can be both smart and stylish.
